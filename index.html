Three Signals That Tell the Engine
Exactly Who Is on the Other End
Most agents send the same follow-up message to every lead and wonder why the conversion rate looks like a flat line. The BehaviorPrint™ changes this. Set it once per lead. It recalibrates every output across all seven modules.
⟳
Signal 1: Response Speed
Did they reply in 5 minutes or 5 days? A lead who responds immediately is wired for engagement and can handle a more direct approach. A lead who took a week is already halfway out the door. The Engine adjusts the pressure, the length, and the tone accordingly. Same situation, different human, different message.
◫
Signal 2: Message Style
Short and punchy, or three paragraphs and a question? People who write in short bursts feel pressured by long messages. People who write in detail feel dismissed by short ones. The Engine mirrors their native communication style so your message feels like it was written specifically for them, because it was.
◉
Signal 3: Platform
A great SMS message lands flat as an email. An email-length message in an Instagram DM gets ignored before the second sentence. Platform shapes register, length, and formality. The Engine writes for the environment this lead actually lives in, not the one you happen to prefer.
